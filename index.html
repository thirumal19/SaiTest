Sai Baba

Sai Baba was an Indian spiritual master who is regarded by his devotees as a saint and a fakir.

He is revered by both his Hindu and Muslim devotees during, as well as after his lifetime.

Worship and devotees

  1. Kakad Aarti (The Morning Aarti) at 4:30 (am)
  2. Madhyan Aarti (The Afternoon Aarti) at 12:00 (pm)
  3. Dhup Aarti (The Evening Aarti) 6:30 (pm)
